How to Apply

1

Prep Your Skin

Start with clean, moisturized skin. Apply foundation and concealer first if using.

2

Apply to Apples of Cheeks

Dot 1-2 drops directly onto the apples of your cheeks using the doe-foot applicator.

3

Blend Seamlessly

Use clean fingers, a damp beauty sponge, or stippling brush to blend outward for a natural finish.

4

Build & Set

Build coverage gradually with additional drops. Set with translucent powder if desired for longer wear.

Pro Tips

  • Less is more - start with one drop and build coverage as needed
  • Tap excess product off the applicator before applying
  • For draping, extend the blush upwards toward your temples
